Welcome Electric Bike FAQ's

How long does shipping take?

We ship within 24 hours of your order! Delivery takes 3–7 business days for most Indian cities. You’ll receive tracking details by SMS and email as soon as your eBike leaves our warehouse.

Can I ride in the rain?

Absolutely! The eBike is IPX5 water-resistant and built for Indian weather. You can safely ride in rain, but avoid deep water or submerging the battery.

Is there an EMI option?

Yes! You can finance your eBike from just ₹2,999/month with 0% interest for up to 12 months via leading banks and PayLater partners. Select EMI at checkout or contact us for help.

What about servicing and spare parts?

We offer doorstep service in 50+ Indian cities. For minor issues, our support team can guide you via video call. All spare parts are available through our website and service partners.

Can I test ride before buying?

Yes! Book a free test ride in select cities. Visit our Choose Your Ride section or contact us to schedule your slot.

What is the warranty and return policy?

Every eBike comes with a 2-year warranty (battery, motor, electronics) and a 10-day no-questions-asked return policy. Ride worry-free!
